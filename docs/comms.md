## üå≤ SkyBlock Specific Hubs

Communities focused entirely on the SkyBlock grind.

::: tip Finding Teammates
Most of these servers have dedicated "LFG" (Looking For Group) channels for Dungeons and Kuudra.
:::

### Subreddits

- [**r/HypixelSkyblock**](https://www.reddit.com/r/HypixelSkyblock/): The most active SkyBlock community on Reddit. Great for memes and progress flexes.
- [**r/SkyblockScreenshots**](https://www.reddit.com/r/SkyblockScreenshots/): Dedicated to high-quality in-game captures.

### Specialized Discords

- **Elite SkyBlock**: Focused on high-level gameplay and veteran players.
  - [Backup Link](https://discord.gg/eliteskyblock)
- **Dungeon Gang**: The go-to spot for finding permanent party members for Master Mode.
  - [Backup Link](https://discord.gg/dungeongang)
- **Ironman Lounge**: A sanctuary for those who like to suffer alone, together.
  - [Backup Link](https://discord.gg/ironman)
